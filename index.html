About Vayleno

Vayleno was created through the innovative collaboration of advanced AI technologies including ChatGPT, Gemini, and xAI. This music player represents the power of collective intelligence, bringing together cutting-edge AI capabilities to deliver a seamless music discovery experience that wouldn't have been possible without their groundbreaking contributions.

Technical Foundation

Vayleno leverages the YouTube Data API, provided completely free of charge by Google. This enables access to a vast library of music content while maintaining compliance with YouTube's terms of service. Our custom interface and design philosophy create a unique user experience, distinct from standard YouTube interfaces, while transparently utilizing this powerful free API service.

Special Thanks To:

  • YouTube API - For providing free access to music content
  • ChatGPT - For creative development assistance
  • Gemini - For innovative problem solving
  • xAI - For advanced AI capabilities
